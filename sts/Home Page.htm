   Speech Therapy Solutions, PLLC
I BRING SPEECH SERVICES TO YOU!
Speech Therapy Solutions, PLLC  is a therapist owned private practice providing speech, language and communication services to Northern Virginia areas.  I welcome your interest in my services and I hope that I can answer any questions you may have and be able to evaluate and treat any speech and language needs you may have.  My company is small, so I am able to give personalized attention to each client.   I work with clients from preschool to adults providing evaluations and treatment of a wide variety of communication and swallowing disorders.  I have experience working in a wide variety of settings; schools, skilled nursing, outpatient facilities, and long term care facilities.   I am a member of the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association (ASHA). , and I am licensed to practice in the state of Virginia. 

I am committed to providing excellent service through standardized testing, standardized learning program, client-centered services that focus on the individual's needs.  My goal is to help you reach your maximized functional potiential. 


Speech Therapy Solutions, PLLC brings the speech and language services to you!  With this fast pace world, many people have very busy schedules and find it hard to fit anything else in their schedule.  I perform sessions within your home or office  environment to make it convenient to you and save you time.   By working in your home or office environment, it insures maximum privacy and better  quality of care.  The home environment allows you  to practice your skills at home and helps with carry-over.
