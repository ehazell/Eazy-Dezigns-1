Our Services

Sunshine Home Health Care is committed to providing high-quality, compassionate care. Our medical training and health care background means that your loved one will be in good hands. Our wide range of services, coupled with the friendly demeanor of our dedicated staff, ensures the best experience possible, for you and your loved one.

Sunshine Home Health Care offers a wide range of plans, each tailored to meet your unique needs. Sunshine Home Health Care is proud to offer the following services:

  • Vital signs monitoring
  • Assistance with mobility
  • Grooming assistance
  • Hygiene support and monitoring
  • Administration of medicine
  • Blood pressure monitoring and care
  • IV administration and monitoring
  • Assistance with lifts
  • Assistance with transfers
  • Bathing
  • Turning
  • Heart Monitoring
  • Toileting
  • Housekeeping
  • Bowel care
  • Assistance with dressing
  • Catheter care
  • Denture care
  • Diabetes Care
  • Wound care
  • Dressing
  • Transportation
  • Exercise and stretching support
  • Feeding
  • Feeding tube support
  • Finger and nail care
  • Incontinence support
  • Monitoring oxygen use
  • Rehabilitative Therapies
  • Respiratory Care
  • Record keeping

Sunshine Home Health Care also supports families and friends with the following services:

  • Care giving education
  • Respite care for family and caregivers

  • Planning of care and evaluation of needs
