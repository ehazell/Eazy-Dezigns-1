Soul Food Entertainment

The Soul Food Entertainment group is a music production division of Eazy Dezigns. Soul Food Ent. provides music production, coordination, management, and artist development for any up and coming artist or music group. We specialize in making sure that the recording artist(s) is able to record and produce music that fits their unique style. From many genres and styles of music in the world today, Soul Food Ent. is designed just as the name entails. To provide a mixture of different music styles or various genres to the artist(s). Soul Food Ent. is a self-owned and managed record company that self promotes from in-house with our own promotion and marketing team. We coordinate and arrange for different promotional shows and concerts to introduce the artist and their upcoming project. Here at Soul Food Ent. there is no task that we are not capable of handling and if we cannot sign a particular artist with our production company then we will make sure that we provide the artist with one of our management teams to guarantee that the artist is taken care of in a professional matter. Not only does Soul Food Ent. produce and promote for the artist but we also have a talented group of writers that work with the producers in writing songs that are specifically made for that particular artist. Soul Food Ent. is known to make unknown artists into the hottest rising stars in the music industry.
