Eazy Dezigns

Eazy Dezigns is a Christian design company
specializing in web and graphics design. We
are fulfilling the need of graphics and designs
to Christian ministries all across the nation. There
has been a need for an alternative to the secular
world. Eazy Dezigns has answered that call to make
sure that ministries spread the gospel across the
world. In many ways, Eazy Dezigns has created
designs and artwork that has brought non-believers
close to God and has helped ministries reach young
people all over. From the suburbs to the urban inner
city youth, Eazy Dezigns has touched their hearts and
help them to build a closer relationship with God.
For it is not only our mission but our motto to make
sure that every person, young or old heart will be
touched by the creative artwork inspired by God and
will find not only truth but also God's love and will
want to build a closer relationship with Him.

                                                  - Ezra "Eazy"Hazell
